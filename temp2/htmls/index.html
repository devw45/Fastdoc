About Us

Our founding partners are a group of medical providers who have decades of experience in emergency room and urgent care settings in hospitals and clinics in the Dallas Metroplex area. FastDoc Urgent Care locally owned and operated clinic by providers who have spent many years in this area. Our children have a grown up attending school at Allen, Frisco and Lovejoy ISDs and we continue to be involved in local activities.

FastDoc is dedicated to providing appropriate, urgent care level services, in a setting that is safe and convenient. We commit to giving you a fair and accurate quote for services, beginning with a screening to make sure that our services are appropriate for your current health care needs. We opened this business with the goal of providing an alternative to emergency room visits and we bring decades of experience in the emergency room to an urgent care. With this approach, we are happy to provide you with a screening and advice on options for evaluation or management, and then you can make an informed decision as to what is best for you.
